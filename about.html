Sonu Deshwal

Sonu Deshwal is a highly esteemed educator specializing in Mathematics and Reasoning. With a strong dedication to excellence in teaching, he has transformed the academic and career trajectories of thousands of students aspiring for competitive exams.

As the founder of Raj Defence Academy, he has been instrumental in guiding students preparing for NDA, RIMC, RMS, Navodaya, and Sainik School entrance exams. His institution is known for its well-structured syllabus, strategic methodologies, and personalized coaching.

Furthermore, he also established Raj Unique Coaching Centre, which specializes in SSC CHSL, MTS, CGL, GDS, HSSC, CET, HTET, and CTET exam preparation. His unique approach, which includes comprehensive study materials and regular mock tests, has led to over 3,500 successful results.

Achievements & Teaching Methodology

Over the years, Sonu Deshwal has developed a reputation for producing top-ranking students in multiple competitive exams. His teaching techniques emphasize conceptual clarity, logical problem-solving, and time management, ensuring that students are fully equipped to tackle the toughest questions.

His Raj Defence Academy has produced numerous officers in the Indian Armed Forces, while Raj Unique Coaching Centre has been instrumental in securing government job placements for thousands. His success-driven approach is backed by an in-depth understanding of the exam pattern and syllabus.

Beyond academics, he is deeply committed to instilling confidence and discipline in students. His motivational lectures and mentoring sessions empower aspirants to push their limits and achieve excellence. His dedication extends beyond the classroom, as he consistently updates his methodologies to adapt to the latest examination trends and requirements.
